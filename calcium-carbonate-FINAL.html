Calcium Carbonate USP Powder

CI 77220
CAS 471-34-1 · Formula CCaO3 · MW 100.09 g/mol

Calcium Carbonate (CaCO3) is a white, odorless, fine crystalline powder. USP grade meets pharmaceutical purity standards for use as an antacid active ingredient, calcium supplement, and pharmaceutical excipient (diluent, binder). In industrial applications, it serves as a filler and pigment extender in coatings, plastics, and rubber; a calcium source in agriculture; a rheology modifier in drilling fluids; and a pH buffer in water treatment. Molecular weight 100.09 g/mol.
