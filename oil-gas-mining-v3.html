Oil, Gas & Mining

Precision-sourced intermediates for energy and extractive operations

RawSource delivers the functional raw materials behind oilfield treatment formulations, gas processing solvents, pipeline protection programs, and mineral beneficiation reagents. We are not a formulator—we are the supplier formulators and operators turn to when they need verified-grade amines, phosphonates, silicone antifoams, flotation collectors, and everything in between, at bulk scale with full traceability.

From amine sweetening units running MDEA at 50 wt% to flotation circuits dialing in xanthate-to-frother ratios, we understand that the right base chemistry—at the right purity, in the right packaging, on the right timeline—is what keeps your operation running. Every shipment includes SDS, TDS, and COA documentation as standard.
